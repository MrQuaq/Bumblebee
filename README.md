# Yeetimus Prime

Yeetimus Prime is 2 stage model rocket that will fly to about 1.2km

Goals:
- use avionics to deploy chutes and take care of ignitions
- film the launch cleanly
- reach an altitude of 1.2km and a speed of mach 0.62

What needs to be made:
- airframe consisting of 2 stages
- 2 motor mounts for TSP F35 motors
- an avionics bay to house pcb, electronics and cameras
- a pcb to act as a flight computer that can take care of:
    second stage ignition
    drogue chute deployment at apogee
    main chute deployment at 200m
